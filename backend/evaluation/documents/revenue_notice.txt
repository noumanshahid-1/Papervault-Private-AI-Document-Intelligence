PROVINCIAL REVENUE AUTHORITY
SERVICES TAX NOTIFICATION

This notification applies to licensed customs house agents submitting goods
declarations through the electronic trade system.

The fixed rate of tax is Rs. 3.000/- per goods declaration. The liability to
pay tax shall be on the person receiving the taxable service rather than the
service provider.

The responsible person shall ensure timely collection and payment of sales tax
on services not later than the 15th day of the following tax period. Records
supporting each declaration must be maintained for five years and produced
when requested by an authorized officer.

Non-payment or short-payment may result in a penalty, default surcharge, and
recovery of tax under the applicable legislation. Returns must be filed using
the authority's electronic portal.

Questions about registration may be sent to registration@revenue.example.
