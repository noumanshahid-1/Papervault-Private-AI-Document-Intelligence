SOFTWARE SUPPORT AGREEMENT

This agreement is between Meridian Systems Ltd. and Example Trading Company.
The service term begins on 1 January 2026 and continues for twelve months.

Meridian Systems will provide application monitoring, incident triage, and
monthly maintenance reports. Critical incidents will receive an initial
response within four business hours.

The monthly service fee is USD 1,200, payable within ten days after receipt of
each invoice. Approved work outside the support scope will be charged
separately only after written authorization from the customer.

Either party may terminate this agreement by giving 30 days' written notice.
A material breach that remains unresolved for fourteen days after written
notice permits immediate termination.

Confidential information must be protected using reasonable safeguards and may
only be shared with personnel who need it to perform the agreement.

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario. Notices
under this agreement must be sent to contracts@meridian.example.
